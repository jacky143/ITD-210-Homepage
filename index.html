Our Story

Mae's Continental Cuisine first opened its doors in 2021 at the stafford market in Stafford, Virginia. With 20 years of experience cooking in the finest restaurants, our chef is excited to present their vision to you and all our guests. Our caring and committed staff will ensure you have a fantastic experience with us.

We refuse to compromise on quality in our restaurant. That's why we source our fresh ingredients from local farmers' markets.Our restaurant is available for private events: weddings, business lunches, dinners, cocktail receptions, and more. We would love to discuss how to be a part of your next event.
